Guatita Recipe

image of guatita

Ecuadorian guatita is a traditional dish of beef tripe stew with potatoes cooked in a delicous peanut sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef tripe washed and cleaned
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 10 cups water
  • 5 cilantro sprigs
  • 4 garlic cloves crushed
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
